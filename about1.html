ONLY NATURAL AND ORGANIC COSMETICS

An organic cosmetic is a product in which at least 95% of the natural ingredients are organically grown, without pesticides or chemical fertilisers and are not genetically modified.

Why Our Products.
COSMETICS WITHOUT SYNTHETICS

Natural cosmetic products contain a high proportion of natural ingredients of plant, animal or mineral origin. They limit as much as possible the use of synthetic ingredients, especially those from the petrochemical industry.
